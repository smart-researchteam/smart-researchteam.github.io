Jean-Michel Bruel

Professor of Software Engineering

University of Toulouse

IRIT Laboratory

Smart Modeling for softw@re Research and Technology

Jean-Michel Bruel is head of the SM@RT team of the IRIT CNRS laboratory. His research areas include the development of software-intensive Cyber-Physical Systems, and methods/model/language integration, with a focus on Requirements and Model-Based Systems Engineering. He has defended his “Habilitation à Diriger des Recherches” in December 2006 and obtained in 2008 a full professor position at the University of Toulouse . He has been head of the Computer Science department of the Technical Institute of Blagnac from 2009 to 2012, and Laboratory Representative for the Toulouse 2 Jean Jaurès University from 2016 to 2020. He is now a member of the Strategic Research Committee of the IRIT CNRS laboratory since 2021.
